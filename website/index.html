Frequently Asked Questions

What about AWS costs?

Lattice pricing is separate from your AWS infrastructure costs. We help optimize your AWS spending through environment-appropriate sizing and cost monitoring, typically saving 30-50% on AWS bills.

How does this compare to hiring a DevOps engineer?

Hiring a DevOps engineer costs $120k-180k/year plus 3-6 months recruitment time, then another 3-6 months for them to build production infrastructure. That's 6-12 months and $150k+ before you're production-ready. Lattice gets you there in 1 week for $299/month, letting you focus your budget and timeline on building your product instead.

What if we need to scale globally?

Traditional global expansion requires senior infrastructure architects ($200k-300k/year) and 12-18 months of complex multi-region setup. Our Growth package includes global deployment patterns and dedicated architectural guidance for $799/month - you'll be live in multiple regions within weeks, not months.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes! Our Growth and Enterprise plans include custom modules and escape hatches for unique requirements. We've never met a use case we couldn't handle with our flexible architecture.

What's included in support?

All plans include infrastructure consultation, health checks, and ongoing optimization. Higher tiers get dedicated architects, faster response times, and comprehensive training sessions.
