# Lattice Market Positioning & Strategy

## Executive Summary

Lattice AWS CDK is positioned as a **production-ready AWS infrastructure platform for startups without DevOps expertise**. Our core mission is to eliminate the need for dedicated DevOps engineers while providing enterprise-grade security, compliance, and cost controls from day one.

"Production-ready AWS infrastructure for startups without DevOps expertise - from prototype to scale with built-in best practices"
